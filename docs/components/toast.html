Toast

Toast is a wrapper to position alert components at the corner of the screen.

Positions

Click to see toast position (shown for 3 seconds):

<div class="toast toast-top toast-end">
  <div class="alert alert-success">
    <span>Message sent successfully.</span>
  </div>
</div>

<!-- Positions: toast-start, toast-center, toast-end -->
<!-- Vertical: toast-top, toast-middle, toast-bottom -->

Multiple Toasts

<div class="toast toast-end">
  <div class="alert alert-info"><span>New message.</span></div>
  <div class="alert alert-success"><span>Saved!</span></div>
</div>
See DaisyUI Toast for more options.
